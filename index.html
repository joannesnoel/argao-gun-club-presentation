ARGAO GUN CLUB

First Meeting Agenda

Safety • Discipline • Integrity • Respect

1. Opening Prayer

Invocation for safety, discipline, unity, and responsible conduct.

Duration: 5 minutes

2. Welcome & Opening Remarks

  • Introduction of officers/organizers
  • Purpose of the Club
  • Core values: safety, integrity, respect, camaraderie, sportsmanship
  • Emphasis on compliance with all Club policies and lawful firearm ownership

Duration: 10 minutes

3. Member Introductions

  • Name, background, experience
  • Encourage a respectful, beginner-friendly atmosphere

Duration: 10–15 minutes

4. Presentation of Membership Guidelines

  • New membership process
  • Probationary requirements (50% shoots, participation, conduct)
  • Endorsement rules
  • Requirements for full-fledged membership
  • Returning/renewal rules
  • Responsibilities of endorsers
  • Attendance expectations and consequences for non-payment
  • Clarification of dues and fees

Duration: 15 minutes

5. Code of Conduct Briefing

  • Core values and expected behavior
  • Respect toward members, staff, and guests
  • Confidentiality and integrity
  • Prohibited acts (unsafe handling, harassment, vandalism, etc.)
  • Disciplinary framework: Light, Serious, and Grave Offenses
  • Mandatory acknowledgment by all members

Duration: 15–20 minutes

6. Firearm Safety Orientation

Led by Range Officer or qualified member:

  • Four Cardinal Rules
  • Range Officer authority and compliance
  • Designated handling areas
  • No alcohol/drugs policy
  • Safe storage, transport, and emergency procedures
  • Range etiquette aligned with the Code of Conduct

Duration: 20–30 minutes

7. Overview of Club Activities & Annual Expectations

  • Monthly shoots and probationary attendance requirements
  • Practices (Tues–Sun schedule)
  • Training sessions and skill development
  • Competitions and events
  • Community involvement and advocacy
  • Updates on policies and legal changes

Duration: 15 minutes

8. Formation of Committees

  • Safety Committee
  • Membership & Screening Committee
  • Events & Logistics Committee
  • Communications & Outreach Committee

(Clarify responsibilities based on the Guidelines & Code)

Duration: 10–15 minutes

9. Administrative Matters

  • Submission of IDs and LTOPF
  • Application forms and initial payments
  • Attendance tracking
  • Voting procedures for future membership approvals
  • Next steps for probationary applicants

Duration: 10 minutes

10. Open Forum

  • Questions on the guidelines and code
  • Suggestions for activities and practices
  • Concerns or clarifications

Duration: 10 minutes

11. Closing Remarks & Next Steps

  • Summary of key agreements
  • Reminders about safety, conduct, and compliance
  • Next meeting date and upcoming shoots/practices

Duration: 5 minutes

Thank You
